Q: Why am I here?

A: To Share my Virtuous Cycle

I have one goal in life: happiness. Everything that I do in life, whether being as small as the color of pen I use and then spanning as grand as my long term career ambitions, contributes to my overall happiness in some way, shape, or form. When everything contributes to the same end goal, it creates a virtuous cycle.

To maintain this cycle, I follow the mantra that life is too short to be doing things that aren't fulfilling, so I try to only do the things that are. The point of this website is to share, with anyone interested, the things that I am passionate about because I think they are awesome and worth sharing!
